Samori

Octavia Project participants completed a month-long workshop series where they brought their imaginary worlds to life in writings, drawings, maps, prints, sculptures, 3D models, online games, and wearable electronics.

Interactive Storytelling
Lakehouse Remix

After playing the other Twine games online I realized how much fun they actually were. My Twine is based on a Wattpad story of Justin Beiber fan fiction called “The Lakehouse”. I wanted to add a piece of code that allows you to input a character’s name, but since my Twine is a horror story I wanted that character to be someone you don’t like—your worst enemy or someone totally made up.

Block Printing
Alien Plants

I thought back to the Venus fly trap I wrote about in our writing warm-up. I wanted to add a different twist to it so I made a combination of the Venus fly trap and a Lily pad.

3D Modeling
Imaginary Worlds

I made up an idea on the spot for the blue foam workshop. I thought about the movie Happy Feet so I made an igloo with a penguin diving board. I also showed the 3D side of the igloo with different levels of blocks. The puddles of hot glue represent melting ice.

Wearble Tech
Futuristic Fashion

I made a superhero inspired mask for my wearable tech project. I learned a lot about circuits and how much the world is changing with technology.
