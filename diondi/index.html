Diondi

This past summer Octavia Project participants completed a month-long workshop series where they brought their imaginary worlds to life in writing, 3D models, drawings, maps, prints, sculptures, online games, and wearable electronics.

Interactive Storytelling
Goldi Locks Remix

My game is based on "Goldylocks and the Three Bears". In the story, the three bears are trying to catch Goldylocks and kill her. She smells the porridge in their home and finds it sitting there on the table and starts to eat it. What inspired me was that I like the story but I wanted to make different endings. Try Diondi's game.

Clay Modeling
Future Cities

My world is in the future, where people are living in clouds and they have birds for pets and they pay no rent! There are animals walking about the city and harming no one in sight. Stores are crowded everywhere and people are shopping back to back. What inspired me about this project is I’ve always wanted to feel the clouds and lay on them and wished that there was no rent to pay.

Wearable Electronics
Futuristic Fashion

In this project I made a wearable glove and arm-band and sewed lights on to them to make them glow. What inspired me to make this project was a writing prompt about Beyonce and Jay-Z in space and what space suits they might wear for a cosmic performance. I sewed the fabric together with the lights and conductive thread to make wearable technology.

Photography
NYC Imagery

For this project we took photos of something relatable to New York. Like the street signs, cars, buses, and maybe people. The photos came out really beautiful. The images were really eye catching and we were all amazed! What inspired this project was that I love taking pictures and making different images. But now I’m really inspired to take pictures of New York.
